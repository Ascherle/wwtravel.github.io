Angela Scherle

I was born and raised in southern Germany. I grew up in a religious town close to Munich, but thanks to my grandparents, who owned a farm, I also got insight in the rural life as a kid, something that always intrigued me. I'm generally no fan of big cities, although I do enjoy visiting them sometimes.
Ever since I can remember I have been a fan of animals of all kind, and whenever I'm on holidays in a foreign country, I like to search for indigenous species, may it only be little lizards or bugs.
Currently I'm studying Digital Media Design in Weingarten, Germany, something that has interested me since I was a little kid. Besides design-related work I like listening to music, hiking in the woods, or horse-riding. I also like traveling, even though the student life doesn't allow you to journey too much. Germany is a beautiful country, but I've never come around much and there is yet so many places I want to see, so many countries I want to visit.
